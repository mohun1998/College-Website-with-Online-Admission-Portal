The College
The rapid growth of College of Computers Communication (CCC) in just a span of 16 years of its existence reflects our unparallel performance in the field of academics. The key factors responsible for the meteoric rise of the college have been its excellence in infrastructure facility, teaching methodology, efficient faculty, efficient placement division, up-to-date library facilities, latest software facilities and above all the performance of our student in all areas.

About us
College of Computers & Communication is the first college to receive the ISO 9001:2000 certification as it has been found to confirm the Quality Management system standards because of its quality education, highly proficient staff, well equipped library, best teaching aids, best results, advance labs & overall performance. It primarily established to impart computer education to the prospective students of the region. Within an year of establishment in 1998, the college managed to achieve the status of a private college by the department of higher education, Madhya Pradesh.
