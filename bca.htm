The computer is widely recognized as the most influential invention of the 20th century. Having become a central feature of our everyday life, computers are everywhere. An education in computing will allow you to apply your knowledge, skill, and creativity to diverse fields of endeavour, covering the full range of human activity from business to arts and from environmental science to the entertainment industry.
